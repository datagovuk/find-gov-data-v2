Making your data findable

The most efficient way to make your data discoverable, is to provide enough information to the global search engines that they know which of your web-pages point at data.

The best way to do this is to use the schema.org markup for datasets. To achieve this GDS has provided a set of tools to make it easier for you to supply the required information.

Tools

You can use the following tools to extend your current CMS or Data Management System and make the required information available to search engines. If your platform is not show below, or you require further guidance on how to implement schema.org on your site, please get in touch

  • CKAN extension
  • Drupal extension
  • Wordpress plugin
